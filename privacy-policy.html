Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2024

Introduction

Orthodox Journey ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Orthodox Journey mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Orthodox Journey is designed to respect your privacy. The App does NOT require you to create an account or provide personal information to access its core features.

Optional User Account:
If you choose to create an account for enhanced features (such as bookmarking favorite periods), we may collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and recovery)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Authentication credentials

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics (which screens you visit, how long you use the App)
  • Crash reports and error logs (to improve App stability)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your precise location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity outside the App
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Improve user experience and App performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance content
  • Communicate with you about App updates (if you have an account)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Reading history and preferences
  • Bookmarked periods (if using offline mode)
  • App settings and customizations

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you create an account, the following data may be stored on our secure servers:

  • Account credentials (encrypted)
  • Bookmarked periods and notes
  • Sync data across devices

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Services

Orthodox Journey may use the following third-party services:

Expo (Development Platform)

  • Used for app distribution and updates
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

Sentry (Error Tracking)

  • Collects crash reports and error logs
  • Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

These services may collect limited technical information to provide their functionality. We do not share your personal information with these services beyond what is necessary for App operation.

Children's Privacy

Orthodox Journey is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and crash reporting in App settings

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 30 days for debugging purposes
  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the App

International Users

Orthodox Journey is available worldwide. If you access the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (if you have an account)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
